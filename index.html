Controls


W = Forward A = move left S = Move back D = move right
LMB = fire
Space = Powerup


Basic info


This game is based off of the top down shooter game galaga. using the ships and alien ships as references and adding a few twists of our own.
By using W,A,S, and D, you can pilot the ships and by pressing SPACE you can fire the cannons at the enemys trying to attack you and earn points once you destroy them.
